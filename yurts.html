The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?

Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off he ground. Each yurt has canvaas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.

How are the yurts furnished?

Each yurt is furnished wiht a queen sized bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. The luxury camping experience also includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.

What should I bring?

Bring a sense of adventure and some time to relax. Most guests also pack comfortable walking shoes and a plan to dress for chanign weather with layers of clothing.
